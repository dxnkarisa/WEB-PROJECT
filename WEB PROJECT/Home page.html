Welcome to the Wildlife Sanctuary. Our animal sanctuary is a safe place where animals can live happily. We take care of many animals, including cheetahs, white-bellied birds, hyenas, dik-diks, elephants, desert warthogs, black-backed jackals, and bat-eared foxes.


  • Cheetah: The fastest animal on land.
  • White-Belly Bird: A small, colorful bird.
  • Hyena: A strong animal that helps keep nature clean.
  • Dik-Dik: A tiny and shy antelope.
  • A big and smart animal that loves water and mud baths.
  • Warthog: A tough animal that lives in dry areas.
  • Jackal: A clever and quick hunter.
  • Fox: A fox with big ears that eats insects.

We help these animals live safely and teach visitors about them. Come visit us and learn more about these amazing creatures!
